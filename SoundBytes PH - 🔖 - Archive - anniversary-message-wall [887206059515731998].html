Sel 14-Sep-21 04:43 PM
I stumbled upon this server by pure chance as a person who was about to make the saddest decision of their lives. It was a hot night in December where I was about to buy the Legendary Mithi from , as I pressed checkout, I asked myself: "Are these even worth it?" then I contemplated the question till I asked over in the zFault server about it, the wonderful people there said it was an absolute piece of garbage IEM and that I should save my hard earned 851 pesos, that moment I was saved by a man named @Pepegya. The kind man even goes out of his way to invite me to a brand new server, a promised land for Pinoy audiophiles if you can say, and that server was Audiogon https://discord.gg/Q7zhrBFaKb... At first I thought little about the server, even had a few clashes with @Alexios , but as I got more involved with the hobby, I started to frequent the server more. Lots of wonderful, bright individuals who are willing to help with a multitude of things like math, PCs, keyboards, games, and most especially, what binds us all to this God-forsaken server of shitposters, bold-enjoyers, and normal people: Hi-Fi Audio. It's been a year since I've been invited to this server, and I don't regret it at all. Sobrang raming memes and taehan sa #drunk-general pero doon ang totoong #lounge, a place where you could vent your anger to people who'd listen, a place where you can ask for gcash money (pero siyempre babayaran mo pabalik ), and a place where we all talk about our niche hobbies and peculiarities we dare wouldn't talk about in #lounge I believe that music should be enjoyed by everyone, even better if you'd actually be able to connect with other people in the hobby. Stay safe sainyong lahat, putang ina niyo. Credits to boss @legit alt ni perseus for making it all happen in that humble night in September of last year. tite!
